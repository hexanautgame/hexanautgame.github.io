Contact Us

Thank you for your interest in Hexanaut Game! As this website is a static page hosted on GitHub Pages, we don't have a traditional contact form or email address.

However, we welcome your feedback and suggestions! Here's how you can reach us:

GitHub Issues

The best way to contact us is by creating an issue on our GitHub repository. This allows us to track and respond to your inquiries effectively.

To submit an issue:

  1. Visit our GitHub repository: https://github.com/hexanautgame/hexanautgame.github.io
  2. Click on the "Issues" tab.
  3. Click the "New issue" button.
  4. Provide a clear and detailed description of your issue or suggestion.
  5. Click "Submit new issue."

We appreciate your understanding and look forward to hearing from you!

Please note that due to the nature of this project, response times may vary. We will do our best to address your inquiries as quickly as possible.
